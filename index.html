About

Aspiring to build sustainable futures for all

I work at the intersection of climate action, governance, and community systems — translating complex policy frameworks into implementation that is practical, equitable, and lasting.

My career spans research, program design, and field implementation across South and Southeast Asia. I engage governments, multilateral institutions, and grassroots communities alike, believing that durable climate solutions require coherence across all scales.

I am particularly drawn to questions of institutional design: how do we build the administrative capacity, financial mechanisms, and political legitimacy necessary for communities to lead their own climate transitions?

"The most durable climate solutions are those that communities don't just participate in — but own."

Aspiration

To contribute to the body of evidence and institutional practice that makes climate governance genuinely responsive to the people most affected by it — and to do so with rigour, honesty, and a long view.
